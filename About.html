Our Mission

E-Waste Facility Locator is committed to reducing electronic waste by making it easy for individuals and businesses to find environmentally responsible disposal and recycling options for their electronic devices.

Our Solution

Our solution provides a user-friendly platform that connects users with nearby e-waste facilities, helping them properly dispose of their electronic devices, thereby reducing environmental impact.

How We Connect Areas

We use advanced geolocation technology to match users with the nearest e-waste facilities, making the process as convenient as possible for everyone in our network.
